# Overview

Q8JAN is designed with a simple and transparent token economy.

The project follows a fixed-supply model with no inflation, no hidden minting, and no transaction taxes.

The objective is to provide a predictable digital asset that is easy to understand and suitable for long-term ecosystem development.

# Supply Model

Q8JAN has a permanently fixed maximum supply.

No additional tokens can ever be created after deployment.

This approach protects holders from inflation caused by future token issuance.

# Burn Mechanism

Q8JAN supports voluntary token burning.

Burning permanently removes tokens from circulation by sending them to an unrecoverable blockchain address.

Burning is optional and reduces the circulating supply over time.

---

# Transaction Policy

# Economic Philosophy

The token economy is intentionally simple.

The project avoids unnecessary token mechanics that may reduce transparency or increase operational complexity.

The long-term focus is on sustainable ecosystem growth rather than short-term incentives.
