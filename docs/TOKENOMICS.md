# Supply Model

Q8JAN has a permanently fixed supply.

The total supply is created once during deployment and allocated to the deployment wallet.

No additional tokens can ever be minted.

This ensures a transparent and predictable token economy.

* Ownable Administration

# Ownership

The smart contract uses the OpenZeppelin Ownable module for administrative ownership.

The owner cannot mint additional tokens, increase the total supply, or modify the fixed supply.

Ownership may be transferred or renounced in accordance with the project’s governance decisions.
